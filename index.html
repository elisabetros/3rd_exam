About the Nature Fund

We at the Nature Fund believe, that nature is a gift and needs to be preserved and enjoyed as such. We, a group of nature enthusiasts and volunteers, want to create an even greener Denmark, so that everyone, despite of wealth, status or ideology, can enjoy the scent of flowers, tall pine trees or take a long walks on clean white beaches. But we, humans, have long had an exploitative relationship with the nature, where we take what we want and don't give back what we should. That is why we founded The Nature Fund, a non-profit organization that aims to preserve and restore Danish nature.

The Nature Fund is a fundraiser, where everyone can find a project to be a part of or just donate some funds to help us to create a better environment. We identify problem areas or ecosystems in danger all over Denmark and then carefully devise how best to preserve or restore it. Since we were founded, in 2000, we have planted over 1000 trees, and saved more than 50 animal habitats, and these numbers are growing! We as an organization think that transparency is very important when it comes to fundraising, and that is why we don't only say that 100% of your donations go straight to saving nature, but we prove it, by making all our records easily accessible. So, do not hesitate and spend a couple of hours with us on your day off and not only make a difference at the place we all live, but spend a nice day outside, enjoying the nature we all cherish.
