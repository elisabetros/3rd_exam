About the Nature Fund

We at the Nature Fund believe that nature is a gift and as such, deserves to be preserved and enjoyed on it's own terms. We want to create a greener Denmark, so that everyone, no matter their wealth or status can enjoy the scent of flowers, look at beautiful, tall pine trees or take long walks on clean white beaches.. But we humans have long had a exploitative relationship with mother nature, where we take what we want and don't give back what we should. That's why we founded The Nature Fund, a non-profit organization that aims to preserve and restore Danish nature.

The Nature fund is a project based fundraiser. We identify problem areas or ecosystems in danger all over Denmark and then carefully devise how best to preserve or restore those areas. For these projects, you can both donate your money to fund the project, but also your time, by becoming a part of a growing community of volunteers. We as an organization think that transparency is very important when it comes to fundraising, and that is why we don't only say that 100% of your donations go straight to saving nature, but we prove it, by making all our records easily accessible.
